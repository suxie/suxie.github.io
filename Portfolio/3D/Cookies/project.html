Café

Modeled in Maya, Textured in Maya & Photoshop, Rendered in Arnold, Composited in After Effects

This is a scene I created with the help of the Python script mentioned here– the chips were placed over the cookies and then manually adjusted and moved slightly for aesthetics. This shot is meant to be from the café on the first floor of Tian's home.

This café is located in the same spirit town shown here. While that model showed the town center, Tian's "grandmother" owns a dessert shop / cafe in one of the town's side streets. In this shot of the side street, the large red building can be seen in the distance. The home owned by Tian's grandmother is the building labeled with the sign saying "蛋糕城."
